Top three activies to do in Lexington

Visit the Kentucky Horse Park

The Kentucky Horse Park contains many horse shows, horse rides, and horses in general.

Watch a basketball game at Rupp Arena

Rupp Arena is home to the Kentucky Wildcats and are where all of their home basketball games are played.

Go ice skating at Triangle Park

Located in downtown Lexington, this park is transformed into a magical ice rink in the winter and is so fun to play in.
